Welcome to Don Toliver's World

Don Toliver is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter, who rose to fame in the late 2010s. He is signed to Cactus Jack Records, a label founded by rapper Travis Scott. Known for his unique blend of hip-hop and R&B. He gained widespread recognition with his hit singles and collaborations, showcasing his distinctive voice and melodic style. Born in Houston, Texas, Don Toliver's music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, and personal growth.

Album: Heaven Or Hell

"Heaven or Hell" is Don Toliver's debut studio album, released in 2020. The album features a mix of hip-hop and R&B, showcasing his unique sound and style. It includes hit singles like "No Idea" and "After Party," which helped solidify his place in the music industry. The album's production is characterized by atmospheric beats and melodic hooks. This album has received critical acclaim and has amassed millions of streams on platforms like Spotify.
